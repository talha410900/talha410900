## ◆ About me

I'm **Talha Amir**, an **AI full-stack engineer** in **Lahore, Pakistan**, working **remote-first** with startups and product teams across the **US and Europe**.

For **5+ years** I've shipped production web applications — the kind real users open every day, with deadlines, growth targets, and operational reality behind them. I work where **product judgment** meets **technical depth**: frontend architecture, API design, data layers, and deployments on infrastructure I trust.

A major part of my work is **AI in production** — **OpenAI GPT**, **Google Gemini**, and **Anthropic Claude** in real features: streaming, structured outputs, **RAG**, **tool calling**, and **workflow automations** across CRMs, dashboards, webhooks, and scheduled jobs.

When it fits the product, I ship **Web3** too: wallets, token tooling, and on-chain flows on **Solana**, **Polygon**, and **Thirdweb** — complexity behind interfaces people actually want to use.
